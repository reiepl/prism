# PRISM Project Vision

## Vision Statement

Create the world's most complete privacy-first Personal Relationship Intelligence System powered by traditional Chinese metaphysics and modern AI-assisted software engineering.

---

## Mission

Enable individuals to better understand people, relationships, timing, and decision making through structured knowledge instead of superstition.

PRISM should provide transparent reasoning rather than mysterious predictions.

---

## Design Philosophy

PRISM should feel like a professional intelligence platform rather than a fortune telling application.

The emphasis is on:

- Knowledge
- Structure
- Explanation
- Transparency
- Accuracy

---

## Product Principles

### Privacy First

All personal information remains under the user's control.

No cloud dependency is required.

---

### Offline First

Every core feature should work without Internet access.

---

### Explainable Results

Every recommendation should explain:

- Why
- Which rules were applied
- Supporting calculations
- Confidence level

---

### Modular

Every metaphysics system should be implemented independently.

Examples

- BaZi Engine
- Tong Shu Engine
- Date Selection Engine
- Relationship Engine

---

### Extensible

Future metaphysics systems can be added without redesigning the application.

---

## Success Criteria

A user can:

- Store people securely
- Understand a person's BaZi
- Compare relationships
- Select suitable dates
- Understand why recommendations are made
